The Influence of Computer Science on Education

Computers are...

Incredible tools for learning

Students are able to take notes, read documents, watch videos, and search for solutions all on one device.

Also great for teaching

Computers enable teachers to assist students, grade assignments, and keep track of student performance.

Digital Avenues of Learning

Computers have drastically changed the way we learn. The internet offers an endless source of information and knowledge, while personal devices serve as great tools for academic tasks.
