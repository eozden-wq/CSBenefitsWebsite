Computer Science - Education

Computers are...

Incredible tools for learning

Students are able to take notes, read documents, watch videos, and search for solutions all on one device.

Also great for teaching

Computers enable teachers to assist students, grade assignments, and keep track of student performance.

Digital Avenues of Learning

Computers have drastically changed the way we learn. The internet offers an endless source of information and knowledge, while personal devices serve as great tools for academic tasks.

Throughout the years, countless pieces of software have been developed with the purpose of improving education.

Examples

  • Kahoot - Everybody loves a good Kahoot quiz
  • Microsoft Teams - Student-teacher communication has never been easier
  • Google Docs - Simple real time collaboration
  • YouTube - Obscure tutorials hard carrying 84% of university students' grades
  • Khanacademy - Teachers can teach without teaching! Sit back and get paid for doing absolutely nothing!
  • ChatGPT - Literally writes every essay in existence

But that's not all...

We are no longer bounded by physical limitations. (i.e: someone living in rural Europe can easily learn about Antarctica so long as they have an internet connection).

In the past, the teacher was nearly the only source of information. To do additional studying, you had to buy books or go to a library.

Now, we have access to a near infinite number of sources for information
